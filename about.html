Our Story

Bad Decisions was born from a simple belief: what you wear should reflect who you are, not who you're supposed to be.

Founded in 2025, we started as a small team of designers and streetwear enthusiasts who were tired of seeing the same generic hoodies everywhere.

Today, we're proud to create pieces that help our community express their individuality while never compromising on quality or comfort.

Our Mission

We create premium streetwear that combines bold design with uncompromising quality. Every piece is crafted for those who dare to be different.

Our commitment extends beyond fashion – we're building a community of individuals who aren't afraid to make their own choices and stand by them.
