Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work properly, improve performance, and provide information to website owners.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Make the website function correctly
  • Remember your preferences (such as language)
  • Understand how the website is used so we can improve it
  • Help protect the website and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We may use the following categories of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for basic website functions (e.g., security, page navigation).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices (e.g., language selection).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site usage and improve performance.
  • Marketing Cookies (if used): Used to deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign performance.

If we use marketing cookies, we will only do so where required by law with your consent.

4. Cookies Set by Bad Decisions

Examples of cookies we may use include:

  • Cookie Consent: Remembers whether you accepted or declined cookies.
  • Language Preference: Stores your selected language to keep the experience consistent.
  • Cart Storage (local storage): Saves your cart items on your device so your cart persists between pages.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some features or services may rely on third-party providers (for example, analytics or payment processors). These providers may place cookies on your device when you use our website. We do not control these cookies, and their use is governed by the third party’s privacy policies.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers let you:

  • See what cookies are stored and delete them
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies

Please note: blocking some cookies may impact website functionality and your shopping experience.

7. Cookie Consent on Our Website

When you first visit our website, you may see a cookie banner asking you to accept or decline cookies. Your choice is stored so we can remember your preference for future visits.

If you want to change your choice later, you can clear your browser cookies/site data for this website and refresh the page to see the banner again.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.
